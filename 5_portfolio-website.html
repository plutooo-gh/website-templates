About Me

I believe the best design is invisible — it just works, and it just feels good.

I'm Mira — a Vienna-based creative director with 8 years of experience working with startups, agencies, and global brands. I specialize in brand identity, UX strategy, and art direction.

My process is rooted in listening first, designing second. Every project starts with a real conversation about what you're trying to say — and who you're saying it to.
